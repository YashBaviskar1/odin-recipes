Chicken Biryani

Description

Biryani is a popular rice dish of indian subcontinent. it consists of chicken with yoghurt and blend of spices typically made using basamti rice.

Ingridents

  • Bay leaf : 1 to 2
  • star anise : 1
  • green cardamoms : 10 to 12
  • black cardamoms : 1
  • two inch piece cinnamom : 2 (2 grams)
  • fennel seeds : 1½ teaspoon
  • black pepper : ¼ to ½ teaspoon
  • cloves : 8
  • caraway seeds (or cumin) : 1 teaspoon
  • mace : 2 strands
  • grated nutmeg : ¼

Steps

    Fried Onions
  1. heat oil in pan.
  2. combine thinly sliced onions in large bowl.
  3. Dust off excess cornstrach and fry onions for 7-8 minutes or until deep brown and drain oil from paper towel. fry onions in batches to prevent overcrowding
  4. Add milk to a bowl and heat it for 40 seconds, then add saffron strands and stir it. set it aside
    5. Marinate Chicken
  5. clean and wash chicken pieces
  6. Combine the rinsed chicken pieces with lemon juice in a large whisking bowl. Toss to combine. Cover and refrigerate the chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  7. after some time, add yogurt, birayni spice blend and ginger-garlic paste
  8. Add fried onions, mustard oil, cilantro leaves, and mint leaves to the chicken.
  9. Mix and toss the chicken to coat in tis marinade. Rub the marinade all over the pieces.
  10. Cover and allow it to rest for an hour.
    11. Rice Preparation
  11. pre-soak the rice for 20 minutes A minimum of 20 minutes of soaking time is recommended to achieve the best results.
  12. Add water to large pan with tight fitting lead. Flavour the water with whole spices, cumin seeds and salt
  13. Add rinsed rice to water. Simmer the rice for 10 minutes, uncovered.
  14. Gently mix the rice and parboil it for 4-6 minutes in hot water.When the cooking time has elapsed, turn off the heat and let the rice stand in hot water for 4-5 minutes. The rice should be 70% cooked.
  15. drain the water from the rice
  16. discard the whole spices from rice and keep the rice warm
    17. Cook the chicken
  17. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed Dutch oven. Add chicken and cook.Chicken should only be half cooked
  18. Cook the chicken pieces for 5-6 minutes over medium heat.
    19. Assemble the Biryani
  19. Take a large pan and Drizzle oil or ghee at its base. Add bay leaves and sliced onions to this pan. Saut√© until the onions are translucent.
  20. Add half-cooked chicken to the pan.
  21. Layer it with parboiled rice and drizzle a generous amount of ghee over it.
  22. Layer this delicious biryani with chopped cilantro, mint leaves, and fried onions. Drizzle the saffron milk mixture overtop.
  23. Finally, add a dash of garam masala. Sprinkle a tablespoon of water and seal the pan.
  24. Cover the pan and seal the cover with a cloth or kneaded dough. cook for 10-15 minutes until rice is cooked thoroughly and is all fluffy and plump.
  25. Remove the cloth and uncover the pan once the desired cooking time is done.
  26. Chicken biryani is all ready. Allow it to sit undisturbed in the pan for 5-10 minutes.s
